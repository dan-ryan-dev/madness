# 🏀 Group Manager: Visual Walkthrough

This guide provides a step-by-step visual orientation for the Group Manager role. Use this to get your group ready for the Madness!

---

## 1. Entering the Madness
Start by signing in at [madness-2026.vercel.app](https://madness-2026.vercel.app). Use the **Magic Link** flow or **Google Sign-in**.

![Sign-In Page](file:///Users/dan/Developer/madness-2026/docs/images/sign-in.png)

---

## 2. Your Admin Command Center
Once logged in, click **Admin Console** in the top navigation. This is where you manage your tournaments and groups.

![Admin Dashboard](file:///Users/dan/Developer/madness-2026/docs/images/admin-dashboard.png)

---

## 3. Customizing Your Group
In the **Edit Group** view, you can manage your 8 players. 

- **Edit (Pencil):** Fix typos in names or email addresses.
- **Delete (Trash):** Remove a player before the draft starts.
- **Member Order:** Update the draft position if needed.

![Member Management](file:///Users/dan/Developer/madness-2026/docs/images/member-management.png)

---

## 4. Entering the War Room (Draft Day)
When the draft begins, enter the **War Room**. 

- **Sidebar:** Track the "Snake" draft order and see who is on the clock.
- **Main Board:** Click any available NCAA team to draft them to the current player.
- **Admin Override:** As a Manager, you can pick for any player if they are having technical issues.

![Draft Room](file:///Users/dan/Developer/madness-2026/docs/images/draft-room.png)

---

## 5. The Season Dashboard
After the draft, your group will have a live **Dashboard**. Here you can track standings, see which teams are earning points, and monitor the bracket's progress.

![Group Dashboard](file:///Users/dan/Developer/madness-2026/docs/images/group-dashboard.png)

---

## 💡 Quick Tips for Managers
- **Mobile Friendly:** The draft room is fully responsive—pick teams from your phone if you're on the go!
- **Tiebreakers:** Don't forget to have your players enter their championship score guess after their final pick.
- **Support:** If a pick is made in error, used the **Undo** button (Super Admin only) or coordinate via the group chat.

**Happy Drafting!** 🏀🏆
