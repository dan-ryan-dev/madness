# 🏀 Group Manager: The Ultimate How-To Guide

Welcome to the **Madness 2026** Group Manager role! As a Manager, you are the architect of your group's tournament experience. This guide will walk you through your journey from the first sign-in to the final championship game.

---

## 🔐 1. Getting Started: Sign-In

We use a **"Low-Tech/High-Security"** Magic Link system. No passwords to remember or lose.

1.  Navigate to the site: [madness-2026.vercel.app](https://madness-2026.vercel.app)
2.  Enter your official email address.
3.  Click **"Send Magic Link"**.
4.  Check your inbox (and spam folder) for a link. Click it, and you're in!

> [!TIP]
> **Pro Tip:** If you're using Google, you can also use the **"Sign in with Google"** button for even faster access.

---

## 🛠 2. The Admin Console

Once logged in, you'll see an **"Admin Console"** link in the navigation bar. This is your command center.

-   **Dashboard:** View your active groups and their draft status.
-   **Groups Page:** Manage the fine details of your rosters and tournament entries.
-   **Note:** You *cannot* edit tournament-wide settings (like importing teams or starting games). That's handled by the Super Admins so you can focus on your group.

---

## 🆕 3. Creating a New Group

Before the draft starts, you need a home for your players.

1.  In the Admin Console, click **"+ New Group"**.
2.  **Tournament:** Select "Madness 2026".
3.  **Group Name:** Give your crew a legendary name.
4.  **Invite Players:** Click "Create Group & Invite Players".
    -   Enter names and emails for your 8 players.
    -   *Practice Mode:* You can use fake emails (like `test1@test.com`) for practice runs, but real emails are required for live notifications.

---

## ⚔️ 4. The War Room (Draft Day)

On Draft Day, click **"Enter Draft Room"** from your dashboard.

### 🧭 Orientation
-   **Big Board:** Shows all available NCAA teams.
-   **Draft Order:** The sidebar shows who is "On the Clock." We use a **Snake Draft** (1-8, then 8-1).
-   **Roster Tray:** The bottom bar shows exactly who has which teams at a glance.

### 🎮 Picking Teams
When it's a player's turn, they click a team logo to draft it. 

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Manager Power (The Override):** If a player is stuck on a phone or loses connection, **you can pick for them.** As a Manager, you can click any available team at any time to assign it to the player currently on the clock.

---

## 👥 5. Managing Your Members

Mistakes happen. Here's how to fix them from the **Edit Group** page:

-   **Edit User:** Click the **Pencil Icon** next to a member. You can fix typos in their **Name** or **Email**. This updates their system-wide profile immediately.
-   **Delete User:** If someone drops out before the draft, click the **Trash Icon** to remove them and free up a spot.
-   **The Tiebreaker:** After a player makes their 8th pick, a modal will appear. Ask them for their **Final Score Guess** (Total points in the Championship Game). You can also edit this manually in the member list.

---

## 📊 6. The Group Dashboard

After the draft is complete, your group moves to the **Season Dashboard**.

### 🔍 What's on the Dashboard?
1.  **Group Standings:** A real-time leaderboard of your 8 players.
2.  **Point Breakdown:** See exactly which teams are carrying your players to victory.
3.  **Live Bracket:** A visual representation of the tournament progress.
4.  **Chat/Trash Talk:** Keep the energy high as the upsets roll in!

---

## 💡 Final Pro Tips

-   **Practice Makes Perfect:** Run a practice draft in the "Practice Tournament" before Selection Sunday to get a feel for the Snake timing.
-   **Email Aliases:** If you need to test multiple players yourself, use `yourname+player1@gmail.com`, `yourname+player2@gmail.com`, etc.
-   **Check the Clock:** The draft moves fast! Remind your players to have their "Big Board" open 5 minutes before the start time.

**Good luck, Manager. May the upsets be in your favor!** 🏀🏆
